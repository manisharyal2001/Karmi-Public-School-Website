Our Story

Welcome to Karmi Public School

Established in 1990, Karmi Public School has been a beacon of quality education in our community. Our journey began with a simple mission: to provide accessible, high-quality education to all children.

Over the years, we have grown from a small institution with just a few classrooms to a comprehensive educational facility serving hundreds of students. Our commitment to academic excellence and holistic development has remained unwavering.

Today, we stand proud as an institution that has shaped the lives of thousands of students, many of whom have gone on to achieve great success in various fields.
